BodyParts3D scientific anatomy assets

The OBJ files in this directory were generated from official BodyParts3D
polygon mesh data reduced at 99%.

Attribution required by the source license:
BodyParts3D, (c) The Database Center for Life Science licensed under CC Attribution 4.0 International

Source pages:
https://dbarchive.biosciencedbc.jp/en/bodyparts3d/download.html
https://dbarchive.biosciencedbc.jp/en/bodyparts3d/lic.html

Generated layers:
- bp3d-skin.obj: Skin, FMA7163.
- bp3d-skeleton.obj: Skeleton in vivo, FMA23876.
- bp3d-major-muscles.obj: mobile subset of major visible muscle concepts.
- bp3d-tendons.obj: Tendon, FMA9721.
- bp3d-nervous-system.obj: Nervous system, FMA7157.
- bp3d-major-vessels.obj: systemic arterial tree, systemic venous system,
  pulmonary arterial tree, and portal venous tree.

These files remain educational/visualization assets and are not medical
diagnostic software.
